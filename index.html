Zhiyuan Li (李志远)

 

I am a tenure-track assistant professor in the Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago (TTIC). My research focuses on machine learning and optimization, especially on deep learning theory.

I received my PhD from the Computer Science Department at Princeton University in 2022, where I was advised by Prof. Sanjeev Arora. I was a postdoctoral fellow in Computer Science Department at Stanford University, working with Tengyu Ma. I did my undergraduate study at Yao Class(2013-2017), Tsinghua University.
